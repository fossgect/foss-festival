About FOSS Festival

Our main aim is to FOSSTer students and professionals to the galaxy of ultramodern technology which is done best through lending and contributing to open source.

About FOSS GECT

This pivotal technical club of GECT has always sparked the latest technologies and has planted plenty of innovative ideas inside the tech/ non tech brains of this college. FOSS GECT has impacted the interests of a lot of newbies in this technologically critical era.

Events

TALK SESSIONS

Welcome to our talk show dedicated to the exciting world of open source! We are thrilled to have some incredible guests with us who are passionate about open source and its potential to transform the world. Throughout the show, our guests will be sharing their insights and experiences, discussing everything from the benefits and challenges of contributing to open source projects, to the legal and ethical implications of open source software. So sit back, relax, and get ready to be inspired by the world of open source!

TREASURE HUNT

Attention all adventurers and thrill-seekers! Get ready for an upcoming treasure hunt that will take you on an exhilarating journey through unknown lands and ancient ruins. With clues and puzzles that will challenge your wits and test your skills. So gather your team, prepare your gear, and get ready to embark on a journey filled with excitement, mystery, and adventure. The treasure awaits are you ready to claim it?

HUGGING FACE

Hugging Face is a company that specializes in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and provides state-of-the-art tools and libraries for working with NLP tasks. The Hugging Face workshop is an excellent opportunity for anyone interested in NLP, from researchers and data scientists to software engineers and developers. Participants will come away from the workshop with a solid understanding of the latest NLP tools and techniques and how to apply them to real-world problems.

PROJECT SUBMISSION

Don t you wanna see and get inspired by the budding talents of the people who are just like you but know where and how to look? We present to you this showcase as some medals and trophies that incarnated along the way. Hands down, this epic showcase will get you goosebumps and also a moment for them to be proud of their hard work. See you on the other side.

DEBATE

Are you someone who has the courage and fervor to articulate your opinions in front of any audience? Or are you passionate to talk about open source and its pros and cons? Then this debate competition is for you. We are ready to give you a safe platform to voice your concerns and support your perspective beamed along with the heat of the competition. So,do not strain your throat, we ll see you on the stage.

INFORMALS

Get ready for a one-of-a-kind, informal session which is all about breaking down barriers, letting loose, and having some fun while still learning valuable insights and tips from experts in your field. The focus is on interactive, hands-on activities and exercises that will keep you engaged and entertained throughout the session. So whether you re a seasoned pro or just starting out in your career, the informal session is the perfect opportunity to learn, grow, and connect with others who share your passion and enthusiasm. So come on out and join us for an experience you won't forget!
